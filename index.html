Menstrual Hygiene

Making Low-Cost Pads:

  1. Clean cotton cloth (10"x12")
  2. Fold into 3 layers
  3. Add plastic lining underneath
  4. Change every 4-6 hours

Free Products: Text "PAD [Your Area]" to 88990 for distribution centers
